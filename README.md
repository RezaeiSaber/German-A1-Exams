## üéØ Topics Covered

The exams assess various aspects of German A1 proficiency including:

- **Alphabet & Pronunciation**: German special characters (√Ñ, √ñ, √ú, √ü), diphthongs
- **Articles & Nouns**: Definite and indefinite articles (der, die, das, ein, eine)
- **Grammar**: Present tense verbs, conjugation, sentence structure
- **Vocabulary**: Common words and phrases for everyday situations
- **Reading Comprehension**: Understanding simple texts
- **Writing Skills**: Forming basic sentences and questions
- **Practical Communication**: Greetings, introductions, shopping, time, dates
